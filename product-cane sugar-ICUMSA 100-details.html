Within the last few years, sugarcane has become very important in India's economy and in the world scenario. India is the largest producer of sugarcane in the world. Sugarcane occupies 9.8 mio hectares, that represent about 12% of all cropland in India. India's sugarcane sugar yields have grown at 3% CAGR since late 1970s, that prove numerous technological advancements in sugarcane cultivation, that happened over the last few decades. Yet, we believe that there is still an enormous potential to improve efficiency of the sugarcane production. Plating failures represent one of the main bottlenecks to increase a sugarcane productivity in India.
